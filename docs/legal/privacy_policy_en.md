# Couwang Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

Couwang ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, where such information is stored, and what choices and rights you have.

Couwang does not provide account registration, login, server-based storage, advertising, or user tracking. Coupon and membership information entered or selected by the user is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to the developer's server or any external server.

## 1. Overview

Couwang helps users manage coupons, gifticons, and membership barcodes on their device and receive expiry reminders.

The App does not collect information that directly identifies you, such as your name, email address, phone number, account credentials, or location. The App also does not use your data for advertising, marketing, analytics, or tracking purposes.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

| Category | Collection Status | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Name | Not collected | Not required by the App |
| Email address | Not collected | No login or account registration |
| Phone number | Not collected | Not required by the App |
| Location | Not collected | No location-based feature |
| Advertising identifier | Not collected | No advertising or tracking |
| Payment information | Not collected | No paid purchase feature |
| Usage analytics | Not collected | No external analytics transmission |

Couwang does not collect personally identifiable information.

### 2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information may be entered or selected by you and stored locally on your device for App functionality.

| Locally Stored Data | Examples | Storage Location | External Transmission |
|---|---|---|---|
| Coupon/gifticon information | Coupon name, brand, expiry date, barcode/QR value, memo | Local storage on your device | No |
| Membership information | Membership name, brand, card number, memo | Local storage on your device | No |
| Images | Coupon or membership images selected by you | App storage on your device | No |
| Notification settings | Notification on/off status and reminder intervals | Local storage on your device | No |
| Notification history | Reminder title, message, read status | Local storage on your device | No |

This information is processed only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

## 3. Purpose of Processing

Couwang processes locally stored data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Description |
|---|---|
| Coupon/gifticon management | Displaying saved coupon information in list and detail screens |
| Membership management | Displaying saved membership barcodes and card numbers |
| Image-based input assistance | Helping users register coupon or membership information from selected images |
| Expiry reminders | Sending local notifications before coupon expiry |
| Notification history | Displaying recent reminder history on the device |

The App does not use data for advertising, marketing, tracking, profiling, or external analytics.

## 4. Retention Period

Couwang does not operate a server and does not retain user data outside the user's device.

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the relevant coupon, membership, notification history, or uninstall the App.

| Data | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Coupon/gifticon information | Until deleted by the user or the App is uninstalled |
| Membership information | Until deleted by the user or the App is uninstalled |
| Images | Until the related coupon/membership or the App is deleted |
| Notification settings and history | Until changed/deleted by the user or the App is uninstalled |

## 5. Sharing with Third Parties

Couwang does not sell, share, disclose, or provide personal information or locally stored user data to third parties.

## 6. Service Providers and Processors

Couwang does not use third-party processors to process personal information.

The App does not transmit user data to external databases, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud servers.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You may access, modify, or delete information you saved in the App at any time.

| Right or Choice | How to Exercise It |
|---|---|
| Access data | View coupon, membership, or notification screens in the App |
| Correct data | Use the edit function in the relevant detail screen |
| Delete data | Use the delete function in the App |
| Withdraw permissions | Change photo/camera/notification permissions in device settings |
| Delete all local data | Uninstall the App from your device |

Because Couwang does not store data on a server, the developer cannot remotely access, modify, export, or delete data stored only on your device.

## 8. App Permissions

Couwang may request the following optional permissions to provide App functionality:

| Permission | Required/Optional | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Photo Library/Gallery | Optional | To register coupon and membership images | Selected images remain on your device |
| Camera | Optional | To capture coupon and membership images for registration | Requested only if camera capture is provided |
| Notifications | Optional | To provide local expiry reminders | Local notifications only, no server push |

You may deny optional permissions. Some related features may be limited if permission is not granted.

## 9. International Users, GDPR, CalOPPA, and CCPA

### GDPR

For users in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Couwang is designed according to the principles of data minimization, purpose limitation, storage limitation, and transparency. The App does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer. Locally stored data is controlled by the user on the user's own device.

If you believe personal data is being processed in a way that affects your rights, you may contact us using the contact information below.

### CalOPPA

Couwang does not track users across third-party websites or services. The App does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals because it does not perform cross-site or cross-app tracking.

### CCPA/CPRA

Couwang does not sell or share personal information as defined under California privacy laws. The App does not collect personal information for advertising or profiling. If you have questions about your privacy rights, please contact us.
